Cloak of Evasion

RARITYUncommon
REQUIRES ATTUNEMENTtrue
CARRIED BY
  • Leda
BULKS0.1

Cloak of Evasion is a very light green cloak with a hood and purple edges and the inside. It is very soft and goes to the knee height of an average humanoid and should be tied around your neck. This cloak is made and used by the Order of Flyers.

While attuned to this cloak, whenever you are getting hit by a Ranged Attack, as a Reaction, you can get a +2 bonus to your AC for the Attack, possibly turning a hit into a miss.

U prii

Skinuli smo ga s mrtvog tijela Flyera kojeg su u 4. Sessionu ubili pauci.

Obojili smo ga u smee kako ne bi bio prepoznatljiv u Gondaru.

Proao je kroz mnoge ruke, trenutno ga nosi Leda.
