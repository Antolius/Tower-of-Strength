Omnigod Amulet

RARITYREQUIRES ATTUNEMENTCARRIED BYBULKS
true
0.1

Requires attunement by a Cleric.

This necklace has a golden chain and a golden amulet in a shape of the sun. In the middle of the sun is a ruby and symbols of many deities.

While attuned to this item, you gain an additional use of your Channel Divinity per short or long rest. Additionally, when you use your Channel Divinity ability, roll a d100. If you roll your cleric level or below, you do not expand the Channel Divinity charge.

U priči

Čarobni predmet originalno pohranjen u grobnici sv. Golivera u Eragorovoj crkvi u Rundeku. Od tamo smo ga "posudili" uz obećanje svečeniku Jozefu da ćemo ga vratiti.
